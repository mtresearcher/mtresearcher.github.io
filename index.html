Hi! My name is Prashant Mathur.

I am a PhD student in Human Language Technologies Lab of FBK. Currently in my 4th and the last year of the doctoral study. I spent the last summer as a visiting researcher at eBay HLT labs in San Jose and the summer of 2013 working as an intern at XRCE, Grenoble. My research interests include Statistical machine translation, domain adaptation, machine learning and making useful industrial products. I received my Bachelor and Master degrees from International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad(India). Born in Ajmer and brought up in Jaipur, I did most of my schooling in St. Anselms and later from St. Johns Kota (Raj.).

You can follow me on Twitter, git my code at GitHub, talk professional on LinkedIn, and get social on Facebook.

I am no more looking for an awesome company to work for.. I found one :)
