Hi! My name is Prashant Mathur.

I am a PhD student in Human Language Technologies Lab of FBK. I am in my 4th year of doctoral study. I am spending my current summer as a visiting researcher at eBay HLT labs in San Jose. I spent my summer of 2013 working as an intern at XRCE, Grenoble. My Research Interests include Machine Translation, related stuff to computational linguistics and online learning algorithms. Previously, I got my Bachelor and Master degrees from International Institute of Information Technology, Hyd. where I was a dual degree student.

I am from Jaipur, Rajasthan and I did my schooling there before going to Kota (Raj.) where I finished my high school. Since I am going to spend quite a long time in Europe I plan to travel and a lot. My wish is to visit more east asian and middle eastern cities, to have an experience in all kind of culture, amazing cuisine, awesome people.

You can follow me on Twitter, check my code at GitHub, talk about academic on LinkedIn, and get social on Facebook.
