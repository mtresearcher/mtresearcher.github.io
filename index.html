Hi! My name is Prashant Mathur.

I currently work as a Senior Applied Scientist at Amazon in New York on multiple topics like automatic dubbing, multimodal generative AI, speech translation. In my prior stints, I worked as a researcher at eBay on topics like natural language generation and machine translation. I completed my PhD from University of Trento (Italy) where I was affiliated to FBK (a non-profit research organization). During my PhD, I spent the summer of 2014 as a visiting researcher at eBay HLT labs in San Jose (US) and the summer of 2013 working as an intern at XRCE in Grenoble (France). I received my Bachelor and Master degrees from IIIT, Hyderabad.

You can find me on Twitter, my code on GitHub, professional profile on LinkedIn.
